How to Play

  • Click "Start Game" to begin the challenge
  • Click on any card to flip it and reveal the symbol
  • Try to find matching pairs of cards
  • Match all pairs to win the game!
  • Use keyboard: Space to start, R to reset, Enter/Space to flip cards

Game Features

Improve your memory and concentration with this classic matching game. Perfect for exercising your mind!

Monitor your moves and time to challenge yourself. Try to complete the game with fewer moves and faster time!

Fully keyboard accessible with screen reader support. Everyone can enjoy this memory challenge!
