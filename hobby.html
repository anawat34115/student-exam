Art Techniques & Styles

🎨 Traditional Art

Classic drawing techniques using pencils, pens, and traditional mediums. Focus on line work, shading, and texture creation through physical media.

Perfect for building foundational skills and understanding artistic principles.

💻 Digital Art

Modern digital creation using tablets and software. Enables unlimited experimentation with colors, layers, and effects.

Ideal for animations, character design, and professional artwork production.

🌈 Color Theory

Understanding color relationships, harmonies, and emotional impact. Essential for creating compelling and balanced artwork.

Helps in creating mood, depth, and visual interest in all artistic pieces.
