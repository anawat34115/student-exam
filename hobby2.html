My Reading Journey

Why Reading Matters

Reading is a very helpful hobby for every step of life. It enhances thinking, understanding, and helps gain knowledge while enjoying stories.

Through reading, I explore different worlds, meet fascinating characters, and gain insights into human nature and culture. Each book opens new doors of imagination and understanding.

My Literary Preferences

  • Manga, Manhwa, Manhua - Visual storytelling with compelling narratives
  • Classic novels - Timeless stories that have shaped literature
  • Commentary and historical books - Learning from the past and understanding different perspectives
  • Favorite genres: historical fiction, heartwarming stories, plot twists, and psychological thrillers

Reading Benefits & Impact

🧠 Mental Stimulation

Reading keeps your mind active and engaged, improving memory, concentration, and critical thinking skills. Regular mental exercise through reading helps maintain cognitive health.

📚 Knowledge Expansion

Every book introduces new information, perspectives, and ideas. Whether fiction or non-fiction, reading broadens your understanding of the world and different cultures.

💭 Emotional Intelligence

Reading fiction helps develop empathy by allowing you to experience different characters' emotions and situations. This enhances emotional understanding and social awareness.
